Roamies Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect your name, email address, and any photos or text you choose to share within your travel circles.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to provide the Roamies service — creating and sharing content within private travel groups.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Your content is only visible to members of your circles.

Push Notifications

We may send push notifications about activity in your circles. You can disable these in your device settings at any time.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen. This permanently removes all your data.
